## 双扭线

$$
\begin{align}
&(x^2+y^2)^2=x^2-y^2 \\
令&\left\{\begin{aligned}
&x=r\cos \theta \\
&y=r\sin \theta 
\end{aligned}\right.
\end{align}
$$
